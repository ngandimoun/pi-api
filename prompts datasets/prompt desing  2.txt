As a senior brand strategist, I have deconstructed the [Brand Name] identity into a precise generation prompt. Recreate a highly structured, brutalist, and tech-forward brand identity presentation board consisting of a three by three grid.
 
 The core color palette must feature [specific colors with descriptive qualities]. The central brand name is [BRAND NAME], set in [specific typography description]. The primary logomark is [detailed logo description with geometric specifications].
 
 The aesthetic merges [style 1] with [style 2]. Include the following visual variations in the grid cells: [9 specific, detailed cell descriptions].
 
 Ensure all layouts use strict alignment, high contrast, and [overall aesthetic direction].
The difference is treating AI like a junior designer who needs an extremely detailed creative brief — not a mind reader.
