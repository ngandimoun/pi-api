THE MAGIC OF DETAILS
The settings I calculated for you aren’t random numbers - they’re your key for premium typography.
Here’s how it works:
Tracking / Kerning - the space between letters in your text. 
Negative values (-3% to -7%) in headlines create tight, luxurious spacing.
Leading - the space between lines of your text. 
0.8–0.9 for headlines adds blocky and solid sense, making the headline a strong design element.
